Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-29

ontahood-downloader uses Google OAuth to access your Google Drive with the drive.readonly scope so it can list folders and download thumbnails and/or media that you explicitly choose.

  • No analytics or tracking are included.
  • No data is transmitted to the developer. The app talks directly to Google APIs.
  • Your OAuth token is stored locally on your device (~/Library/Application Support/Ontahood Downloader/token.json on macOS).
